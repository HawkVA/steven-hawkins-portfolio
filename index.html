About Steven

Steven Hawkins is a professional Bass-Baritone Voice Actor specializing in Warm, Sincere, and Dynamic performances. With a versatile range that spans from trustworthy narrations to creature voices for games, Steven delivers broadcast-ready audio that connects with audiences.

Recording from a dedicated home studio, Steven offers fast turnaround times and is available for directed sessions to ensure your project gets exactly the tone it needs.

Studio Specs:
• Microphone: Audio-Technica AT2020
• Interface: Focusrite Scarlett Solo (3rd Gen)
• DAW/Processing: Reaper & iZotope RX 7
• Connectivity: Discord, Zoom, Google Meet
